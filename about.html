About Us

NWA Skywarn

Northwest Arkansas Skywarn was started by Jonathan George, K0JWG in early 2021 after seeing the growing need to provide the region with severe weather coverage. After much thought, teamwork, and document writing by that spring the program set off, just in time for storm season. Now the program is maintained by Jon Williams, K5DVT.
