NWA Skywarn is an organization of weather spotters in Northwest Arkansas. We utilize Amateur Radio and other means to passive timely, pertinent, and crucial reports to the National Weather Service Office in Tulsa. Due to NWA being at the intersection of the three office area (Tulsa, Springfield, and Little Rock) we do often handle reports to those areas.
