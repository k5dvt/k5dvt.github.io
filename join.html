Requirements for being a NWA Skywarn Storm Spotter

(This information is also available in the NWA Skywarn Storm Spotter Guide)

Spotter Requirements
The Spotter should have a core knowledge base of certain weather aspects to understand not only what to report to the NWS, but also to be safe. These requirements will need to be refreshed yearly. More information is available in the Spotter Manual.
  • Taken and pass the COMET MetEd Online Skywarn Spotter training course.
  • Attend at least one Storm Spotter Training class. Classes from other adjacent NWS areas will suffice.
  • Re-register Yearly (See form below)
  • Join at least one communication forum (Amateur Radio, Discord, or our Facebook Page.)
  • Keep a screen-shot or physical list of the latest Reportable Criteria for the Tulsa NWS
  • A Radar of your choice, although we prefer Radar Scope.
Spotter Suggested knowledge
The Spotter can increase their knowledge and skills by taking the other following courses:
  • COMET MetEd Online Skywarn Spotter training course onRadar Signatures for Sever Convective Weather
  • COMET MetEd Online Skywarn Spotter training course onWeather Observing Fundamentals
  • COMET MetEd Online Skywarn Spotter training course onWeather Radar Fundamentals
  • YouTube video - NWS Norman onTopics in Advanced Spotter Training - Basic Radar Interpretation
